Hi, I am Xinkai Wang.
Currently, I am a postdoctoral researcher in the School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, working with [Prof. Minyi Guo](https://cs.sjtu.edu.cn/~guo-my/). I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2026, advised by [Prof. Chao Li](https://www.cs.sjtu.edu.cn/~lichao/index.html). I was a visiting scholar at Ghent University, advised by [Prof. Lieven Eeckhout](https://users.elis.ugent.be/~leeckhou/). I received my bachelor's degree with Zhiyuan honors from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021.

My research area is computer architecture and systems, with a primary focus on efficient and resilient AI infrastructure. I am currently interested in energy-efficient design exploration for agentic AI infrastructure and agentic management for highly-resilient infrastructure. If you are interested in my project details and further collaborations, feel free to contact me. Here is my [Curriculum Vitae](/files/cv-latest.pdf).
